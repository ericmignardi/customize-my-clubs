About Us

My name is Elliott and I have a Degree in Industrial Design, I am an assistant golf professional at the Beverly Golf & Country Club, a CPGA member, and I am a proud Titleist staff representative.

I knew when I was finished school I wanted to combine my creativity along with my love for the game of golf; thus came Customize My Clubs. My journey started in my parents' garage with a work bench, an old wedge, a hammer, some paint and a creative mind.

Back in 2019 I started stamping wedges for friends and family. Since then I have been fortunate enough to stamp wedges for people all around the world, and someday I hope to stamp wedges for the PGA players.
